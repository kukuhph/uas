About Me

Welcome to outdoor equipment rental! Our goal is to help you get the most out of your outdoor adventures by making high-quality gear more accessible and taking the guesswork out of choosing gear. My name is J Rokies and like many of you, the outdoors and big game hunting are my passions. For the last 20 years I have traveled from Asia to Africa in search of hunting adventures. I also enjoy field hunting, teaching archers, and snipers. I've always been passionate about helping others improve their skills and technical knowledge in shooting and sports. I'm a certified dentist, optometrist, precision expert, and backpacker! Over the years, I've spoken to many hunters about gear and equipment options. GetAdventure.com has all the necessities and gear for the outdoors.

Mount Everest attracts many climbers

Posted by Great Adv on August 24, 2023

Mount Everest attracts many climbers, including highly experienced mountaineers. There are two main climbing routes, one approaching the summit from the southeast in Nepal (known as the "standard route") and the other from the north in Tibet. While not posing substantial technical climbing challenges on the standard route, Everest presents dangers such as altitude sickness, weather, and wind, as well as hazards from avalanches and the Khumbu Icefall. As of November 2022, 310 people have died on Everest. Over 200 bodies remain on the mountain and have not been removed due to the dangerous conditions
